## WHAT OUR SITE IS MISSING (gap analysis)

### CRITICAL GAPS (must fix)
1. **No photos** — Every competitor with photos converts better. We have zero. Need: team shots, equipment, truck, job site, before/after
2. **No FAQ section** — American Leak Detection and Benjamin Franklin both have FAQ. Common questions: "How do I know if I have a slab leak?", "Will you need to dig up my yard?", "How much does leak detection cost?"
3. **No DIY diagnostic tools** — American Leak Detection has bucket test + meter test guides. This is brilliant: builds trust, captures SEO traffic, positions as expert
4. **No warranty/guarantee language** — American Leak Detection offers 30-day warranty on detection + 1-year on repairs. We say nothing
5. **No specific technology descriptions** — We list "acoustic" and "thermal" but don't explain HOW they work or WHY they're better than digging. American Leak Detection does this well
6. **Gmail-level email** — Expert Leak Water uses gmail. We don't, but our email (hello@) needs to actually be set up and deliverable

### HIGH PRIORITY GAPS
7. **No "Signs You Have a Leak" section** — Every top competitor has this. Common signs: water bill spike, wet spots, mold smell, running meter, foundation cracks, warm floor spots
8. **No 3-step or process visual** — American Leak Detection has "3 Steps to Solving Your Water Problems." Benjamin Franklin has service feature toggles. We have HowItWorks but it's generic
9. **No service area pages** — Benjamin Franklin has individual pages per service area with unique content. We have a badge grid. Major SEO gap
10. **No cost/pricing transparency** — Nobody shows exact prices but several say "free estimates" and "no trip fee." We need explicit language
11. **No Google review link** — Should link to actual Google Business Profile for social proof

### NICE TO HAVE
12. **Smart home integration mention** — American Leak Detection offers StreamLabs water monitoring. We could mention Moen Flo or similar as an upsell
13. **Insurance/restoration guidance** — Mention that leak damage may be covered by homeowner insurance, offer to work with adjusters
14. **Seasonal content** — Charlotte gets freeze risks in winter. Seasonal leak prevention content
15. **Video content** — None of the competitors have video. First mover advantage

8-10 signs with icons:
- Unexplained water bill increase
- Sound of running water when nothing is on
- Warm or damp spots on floors
- Mold or mildew smell
- Cracks in foundation or walls
- Water meter spinning when all fixtures are off
